Deep-Focus Color Game 2022

Hello Friend!

I have developed a very simple game which helps you enhance your focus and responsiveness.

It's still in an early stage and I would appreciate it if you test it and give me suggestions to make it better.

Important: Since the game is fast-paced, there are a few audio cues to indicate correct, wrong, or missed choices so that the player does not need to get distracted by looking at the progress. You may turn the volume up and use a headphone for a better experience.

The game is very easy to play. (read the text at the top, select the related circle/box)
  1. Press "new game" to start.
  2. Read the text at the top and select the circle that has said color as fast as you can.
  3. You gain scores by selecting the right circle before timeout. Try to score as much as you can.

Hint: Don't trust your eyes! You have to select the color based on what the text says not the highlighting. For example, "Red" is highlighted in green but you have to select the red circle.

If you liked the game please share your thoughts and give a star the project on GitHub.

Hint: you may record yourself while playing the game(recommended)
